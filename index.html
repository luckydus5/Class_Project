About InsightIQ

We are a passionate team of 6 students dedicated to improving mental health support in Rwanda

Who We Are

InsightIQ is a student-led initiative focused on addressing mental health challenges among youth and students in Rwanda. We believe that mental wellness is fundamental to academic success and personal growth.

Why We Started

Mental health issues among students have been overlooked for too long. We recognized the urgent need to create awareness, provide support, and build a community where students feel safe to discuss their mental health challenges.

Our Vision

To create a Rwanda where every student has access to mental health support, leading to improved academic performance and overall well-being.

Our Mission & Problem Statement

Addressing the mental health crisis among Rwandan youth

The Problem

Mental health issues among students in Rwanda are often stigmatized and overlooked, leading to poor academic performance, social isolation, and long-term psychological problems. Many students struggle silently without proper support systems.

Our Solution

We provide peer support, awareness campaigns, and resources to help students cope with mental health challenges. Our approach focuses on destigmatizing mental health conversations and creating safe spaces for students to seek help.

Our Mission

To break the silence around mental health in Rwanda among youth. we aim to create safe, culturally-rooted spaces where individuals can express their struggles, find connection, and inspire a nationwide movement of compassion, awareness, and resilience.

Our Impact

The positive changes we're bringing to the community

Academic Performance

Students report improved focus and better grades after receiving mental health support

Community Building

Creating supportive peer networks where students feel comfortable sharing their experiences

Breaking Stigma

Normalizing conversations about mental health in schools and communities

Early Intervention

Identifying and addressing mental health issues before they escalate
